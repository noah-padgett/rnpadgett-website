Reporting Growth Mixture Models

I am currently wrapping up my work on a project investigating the alcoholic tendencies of adolesences. The data come from a data study in the United Kingdom, and I was brought on the project to help with the analysis. For this study, the aim was to investigate the presence of unobserved heterogeneity on the growth trajectories of this construct of ‘alcoholic tendencies.’ For this analysis, I implemented a Growth Mixture Model (GMM).
